Detailed Description

DelaySwitch filters a binary filter that may be noisy and creates a binary signal that is definitely not noisy but may be a little delayed.

Does this by creating a MovingAverageFilter of all previous binary inputs and doesn't change output until the moving average of input is way above or way below 0.5.
