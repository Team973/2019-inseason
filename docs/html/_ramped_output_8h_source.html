4  * RampedOutput is a filter that garauntees the maximum rate of change in any
5  * signal is at most a certain threshold. This can be used to ramp drive
6  * output on a root with high CG, to ramp motor output on a flywheel that
7  * might otherwise spike the battery, or as a filter to a sensor where
8  * we know the rate of change of value should always be below a certain
9  * threshold.
10  *
11  * The units for rampRate is whatever is being measured per sec. This class
12  * has an internal clock so regardless of how often it is being called, the
13  * ramp rate will stay the same.
