◆ SetICap()

void frc973::PID::SetICap ( double  icap)

Set a cap on the integral term of PID. This cap will be in the units of error*sec, whatever that means to you. This cap works in absolute terms; so the integral of error will be bound between positive |icap| and negative |icap|.

Parameters
icapThe maximum integral of error that may occur in the units of error*sec.
